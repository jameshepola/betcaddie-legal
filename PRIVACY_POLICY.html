Privacy Policy for Bet Caddie

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Bet Caddie ("the App," "we," "us") collects, how it's used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and the name you provide.

Profile information. You may optionally add a profile photo, a GHIN handicap index, and payment handles (Venmo username and/or Zelle phone or email) so other players can settle up with you after a round.

Round and betting activity. When you play a round through the App, we store the course played, hole-by-hole scores, the bet formats and stakes selected, the players involved, and the resulting balances (who won or lost money, and how much).

Social activity. If you post a round to the feed, follow other players, or react to posts, we store that activity so it can be shown to you and other users of the App.

Challenges. If you post or respond to an open challenge on the Board, we store the details of that challenge (course, stakes, handicap range, and your response).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to: - Operate the core features of the App (scoring, bet settlement calculations, feed, leaderboards, seasons, trips, and related standings) - Show your rounds and activity to other users you interact with (followers, other players in your rounds, or — for feed posts — any user of the App) - Generate optional AI-assisted content, such as suggested trash-talk captions, using a third-party AI provider (Anthropic). Prompts sent for this purpose include round results (scores, names, and dollar amounts) but not your account credentials. - Improve and maintain the App

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Visible to other users: posts to the public feed (including course, results, and any caption you add), your profile name and photo, and your Venmo/Zelle handle if you've added one and it's shown to players you've played a round with.

Third-party service providers: we use Supabase to host our database, authentication, and file storage, and Anthropic to generate optional AI-assisted captions and course lookups. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy and security practices.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Bet Caddie, our users, or others.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile tab. Deleting your account permanently removes your account and associated profile data. Some information you've shared in the context of a shared round (such as a feed post visible to other users, or a round another player recorded) may persist as part of that other user's data, since it reflects a shared event rather than data exclusively about you.

5. Your Choices

  • You can edit or remove your profile information, including payment handles, at any time.
  • You can unfollow other users or stop following you back.
  • You can delete individual feed posts you've created.
  • You can delete your account entirely, as described above.

6. Children's Privacy

Bet Caddie is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

7. Real-Money Wagering Disclosure

Bet Caddie facilitates the recording and calculation of informal wagers between users; it does not process, hold, or transmit money itself. Any money owed is settled directly between users via third-party services (such as Venmo or Zelle) outside of the App. Users are responsible for ensuring that participation in wagering complies with the laws applicable to them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above when we do.
